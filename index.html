Top 5 Expert Tips for Healthy Teeth

Brush Twice a Day - Morning and Night (Before Bed).

Use the Right Toothbrush and Toothpaste - Soft-bristled brush and fluoride toothpaste.

Brush for Two Minutes - Use gentle circular motions and cover all areas.

Don’t Forget Flossing and Rinsing - Clean between teeth and use mouthwash daily.

Visit Your Dentist Regularly - Schedule check-ups at least twice a year.
